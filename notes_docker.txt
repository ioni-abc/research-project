image: prom/prometheus:latest
- `image` is one of two ways to create a container, the other being `build` (when you write your own code)
- prom/prometheus: prom is the official open-source publisher

ports:
    - "9090:9090"
- LAPTOP_PORT : CONTAINER_PORT

volumes:
      - ./prometheus.yml:/etc/prometheus/prometheus.yml
- `volumes`: a live portal between the laptop's hard drive and the inside of the docker container
- it matches the local configuration of `./prometheus.yml` to the internal file path inside
the prometheus vm `etc/prometheus/prometheus.yml`

depends_on:
- prometheus is configured to depend on the 4 other services in order to reach out
and scrape metrics from them. this also ensures that it will wait for them to boot up
first.

environment:
    - COLLECTOR_OTLP_ENABLED=true
- `environment`: is used to set environmental variables inside the container
- `COLLECTOR_OTLP_ENABLED=true`
